Michael Scofield


Michael Scofield is a brilliant engineer and the main character of the TV series Prison Break. He deliberately gets himself incarcerated in a prison to help his wrongly convicted brother, Lincoln Burrows, escape, using his intelligence to outsmart the system.

Michael Scofield, portrayed by Wentworth Miller, is a fictional character from the television series "Prison Break," known for his intelligence, strategic thinking, and ability to escape difficult situations, particularly his elaborate plan to free his brother, Lincoln Burrows, from death row.

Michael Scofield is a determined and resourceful man who meticulously plans a daring prison break to free his brother, Lincoln, from death row. With a complex plan and an intricate tattoo map on his body, he navigates the challenges of prison life while working toward his goal of saving Lincoln.
